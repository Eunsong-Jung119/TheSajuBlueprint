Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The Saju Blueprint ("we", "us", or "our") operates https://saju-blueprint.vercel.app/. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you purchase and use our digital report service.

Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect the following information:

  • Name and email address
  • Birth date, birth time, and birth timezone (required to generate your report)
  • Gender (used for astrological calculation purposes)
  • Payment information — processed securely by Paddle; we do not store card details

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Generate your personalized Saju Blueprint report
  • Send you your report and order confirmation via email
  • Respond to customer support inquiries
  • Improve the accuracy and quality of our service

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Your report and associated birth data are stored securely in our database to allow you to access your report via your unique report link. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Paddle — payment processing. Paddle acts as the Merchant of Record for all transactions. Their privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Supabase — secure database storage
  • Vercel — hosting and infrastructure
  • OpenAI — AI-assisted report generation

Cookies

We use minimal session storage to facilitate the checkout and report generation process. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To make a request, contact us at hello@saju-blueprint.com.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@saju-blueprint.com.
